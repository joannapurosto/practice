Website design for RecRight

RecRight is my employee and we recreated the whole brand in January 2017. The company launched a new software and changed it's name and the website needed to be recreated by then. I made the drawings for the page in Photoshop and based on that our developers created the webpage. We have already made major improvements to the site since the first launch and we will continue to enhance it.

This is one project that has sparked my interest in web development since I had to get to know the principles and restrictions of website development when I was designing the page. We are currently using Prismic for content management so any coding isn't required from our marketing department.
